Who we are?

We are BeerCo, a small local brewery in Ukraine that produces quality craft beer. Our team of experienced brewers carefully crafts each batch using only the finest local ingredients, resulting in a range of flavorful and aromatic brews. From crisp pilsners to rich stouts, there's something to suit all tastes. Come visit us and enjoy a warm and welcoming atmosphere, perfect for relaxing and savoring the flavors of Ukraine's thriving craft beer scene.

What we produce

Citra

One style, one recipe, but in each case a different American hop. We added the selected variety during boiling and cold hopping.

Ipanema

Light, airy, playful, unexpected, and imaginative, new every time it's played, this beer is a mood-boosting drink for improvisers.

Golden Ale

The blend of American hops perfectly balances the malty character of this beer. Bronze medalist at the International Beer Challenge 2017, 2018.

Milk sout

The mixture of oat and rye malts, caramel and well-roasted barley, gives it a luxurious chocolate taste with a hint of bitterness.

Hoppy Lager

Not just a lager, but a bit brighter, not as flat, with a touch of aromatic hops and a little cold hopping just in case.

Captain Salt

Pleasant salty taste, flavored with notes of lemon and hoppy drink. Goes well with fish dishes and seafood.

De Blanche

Wheat-wheat, coriander-coriander, cedar-cedar, a fresh summer wind, with lemon and orange zest.

S Daughter

Sharp as a blade of a razor, ginger. White as the face of the first beauty, rice. Light as the touch of a spring breeze, hop.
